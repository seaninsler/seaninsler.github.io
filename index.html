How I work

I’m strongest where requirements are unclear, constraints are real, and the product actually has to work.

  • I start with the underlying system, not the UI. If the mechanics don’t work, nothing else matters.
  • I translate ambiguous business problems into clear product shapes teams can build.
  • I’m comfortable across strategy and execution — roadmaps, backlogs, edge cases, delivery.
  • I spend as much time deciding what not to build as what to build.
  • I optimize for adoption and correctness (not hype): data flow, compliance, permissions, ops realities.
